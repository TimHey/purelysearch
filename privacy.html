Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PurelySearch LLC ("PurelySearch," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website, contact us, or opt in to our text messaging program. PurelySearch LLC is organized in Delaware and based in Hilton Head Island, South Carolina.

Information we collect

  • Contact information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and mobile phone number, when you contact us or fill out a form.
  • SMS opt-in information, including your mobile number and the fact that you consented to receive text messages.
  • Communications you send us by email or text, and our replies.
  • Basic website data such as pages visited, collected through standard web analytics.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide our consulting services.
  • To send you text messages you have opted in to, including scheduling, follow-ups, and project updates.
  • To communicate with you about your engagement with us.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website.

SMS messaging and consent

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in information with any third party for their own marketing purposes. Your consent to receive text messages is never shared with third parties for their marketing.

When you opt in to our text messaging program, we use your mobile number only to send the messages you agreed to receive. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, or reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. See our Terms of Service for full messaging terms.

How we share information

We share your information only as needed to run our business, and never your SMS opt-in data for third-party marketing:

  • Service providers who help us operate, such as our text messaging provider (Twilio), email, and analytics tools. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound to protect it.
  • Legal reasons, if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our services, communicate with you, and meet legal obligations. You can ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your choices

  • Opt out of texts any time by replying STOP.
  • Ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal information by emailing us.
  • Unsubscribe from emails using the link in any email or by contacting us.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted, and the "Last updated" date will reflect the revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information? Email us at tim@purelysearch.com.
